Why Bariki?

Bariki is a blockchain-based fundraising platform designed to facilitate secure and transparent charitable donations.
The service harnesses the power of blockchain technology to offer both donors and beneficiaries a reliable, trustworthy system for managing and tracking contributions.


Transparent


Leveraging the immutable and decentralized nature of blockchain technology , every transaction and contribution made through our service is recorded on a public ledger, accessible and verifiable by anyone, anytime.


Safe


Our rigorous security protocols are in place to safeguard sensitive information, providing our users with the assurance that their donations and personal data are handled with the utmost confidentiality and care.


Efficienct


The Bariki Blockchain Foundation excels in operational efficiency, eliminating unnecessary intermediaries and utilizing blockchain technology to facilitate instantaneous transactions.


Fast



By leveraging blockchain technology, Bariki reduces transaction times, ensuring that a greater portion of each donation reaches its intended recipients swiftly.
